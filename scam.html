How to Identify Common Scams

Learn the red flags and key indicators that can help you spot a scam before it's too late. Your vigilance is your best defense.

Urgency & Pressure

Scammers often create a sense of urgency, pressuring you to act immediately without thinking or consulting others.

Unusual Payment Methods

Requests for payment via gift cards, wire transfers, or cryptocurrency are common scam tactics, as they are untraceable.

Suspicious Links/Attachments

Be wary of links or attachments in unexpected emails or messages. They can lead to phishing sites or malware downloads.

Unsolicited Contact

If you didn't initiate contact, be suspicious. Verify the caller's identity through official channels, not information they provide.

Poor Grammar/Spelling

Many scam communications contain noticeable grammatical errors or spelling mistakes, a sign of unprofessionalism and fraud.

Requests for Personal Info

Legitimate organizations rarely ask for sensitive personal or financial information over the phone or via unsecure email.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about scams, how VerifyCall helps, and what to do if you've been targeted.

What is a scam call?

A scam call is an unsolicited phone call made by fraudsters attempting to deceive you into giving them money, personal information, or access to your computer. They often impersonate legitimate organizations like banks, government agencies, or tech support.

How does VerifyCall protect me from scams?

VerifyCall allows legitimate organizations to register and verify their caller ID, displaying a "Verified" badge or unique information on your screen when they call. This helps you instantly recognize genuine calls and differentiate them from fraudulent ones.

What should I do if I receive a suspicious call?

If you receive a suspicious call, do not give out any personal information or money. Hang up immediately. You can then try to verify the call by contacting the organization directly using a known official phone number (not one provided by the caller).

Can I report a scam to VerifyCall?

Yes, we encourage you to report any scams you encounter using our "Report a Scam" section on this page. Your reports help us track new tactics and share information with our community to prevent others from falling victim.
